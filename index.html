Projects
Interleaved Bi Directional DC-DC Converter

Interleaved Bidirectional DC-DC Converter, enables efficient two-way power transfer between low and high voltage sources using interleaved switching for reduced ripple and improved performance. It's ideal for electric vehicle applications, ensuring smooth operation during both charging (boost) and regenerative braking (buck) modes.

Smart Energy Meter

It involves real-time monitoring and control of electrical parameters using an ESP32 microcontroller, current and voltage sensors, a relay module, and cloud connectivity via Blynk. The system allows users to remotely monitor voltage, current, power, and energy consumption, and control loads through a mobile app, promoting efficient and safe energy management.

LPG Gas Level Monitering & Leakage Detection System

The LPG Gas Level Monitoring & Leakage Detection System continuously tracks the gas level in the cylinder and detects potential leaks using sensors like load cells and MQ-2. It ensures safety by triggering alerts and notifications through IoT-based platforms, helping prevent accidents and manage gas usage efficiently.
